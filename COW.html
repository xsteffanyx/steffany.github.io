Swallowing Bond

2021 - BEAL ART YEAR 2

This piece is an in-depth analysis of concept in ceramics, encapsulating transforming the body and the timelessness of ceramics. It portrays the story of a two-headed cow, as a sicken birth, a deformed baby, a poor short-lived anathema. Inspried by the poem The Two-headed Calf by Laura Gilpin

The Two-headed Calf

By Laura Gilpin
Tomorrow when the farm boys find this
freak of nature, they will wrap his body
in newspaper and carry him to the museum.
But tonight he is alive and in the north
field with his mother. It is a perfect
summer evening: the moon rising over
the orchard, the wind in the grass.
And as he stares into the sky, there
are twice as many stars as usual.

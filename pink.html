Process

Condensing Ideas: After doing research in yin and yang and dissecting it with the lyrics in my songs I started with some ideas with how to translate the feelings in the songs visually. Experimentation can be as limiting as it is unlimited, so to give me direction to the treatment of type in this assignment I chose to ground my ideas to a few relationships between the lyrics of the songs and the concept of yin and yang. Harmony vs. Dissonance. Traditional vs. Modern. Motion vs. Stillness.

Type Inspiration: I was greatly inspired by the use of text in classic and modern tattooing, expressing type on skin and this form of transferring ink on a human surface works well with the concepts I have for the poster. I use this style of compositions and try to stretch them to their limit in readability, colour, and form. I plan to use various type sizes, some large/grandiose - letting the small text become texture from afar and some small/intimate text - to allow the viewer to digest each letter form along with the feel, colour, and contrast of the paper.

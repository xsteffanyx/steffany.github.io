Hello! My name is Steffany Medrano and this is my cat Junior. I am an aspiring graphic designer currently pursuing a Bachelors of Design at OCADU (Ontario College of Art and Design University) and I am based in Toronto. My love for art and design begin at an early age living through the boom of social media, where I edited photos and made posters on MSPaint.

My work is informed around my childhood experiences with graphic design, what adroned the early 2000's, from magazine to video games to album covers, will always have a special place in my practice. I use this background with a modern recontextualization to create my own nook in the design field. I've always beielved culture works in pendulum swings and I am obessed with the never-ending cycle of the new becoming old and the old becoming new.

Through years of work I have honed my craft in key Adobe softwares, Figma, and even Canva. I am knowledgeable in various forms of printing for physical media and outputs for digital media. I am dedicated to my designs and make my own illustrations and photography for many of my school led projects, I yearn to create.

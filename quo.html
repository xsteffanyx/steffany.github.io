Poetry Booklet

2023 - OCAD YEAR 2

As part of a larger project exploring the emotional and visual expression of poetry, I designed and produced an accordion book of my selected poem, Quo Vadis by Carmen Boullosa with a short interview of Katie, my cousin, who also provided the voice for the accompanying video. I then transcribed that interview, integrating excerpts into the book alongside the full poem. Using InDesign, I created a 3-fold layout that included an introduction, typographic treatment of the poem in both original and the translated version, and original visual elements that echoed the tone and themes of the poem. The result was a cohesive printed piece that complements the motion design component and presents a personal, layered interpretation of the poem.

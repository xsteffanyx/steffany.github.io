In this assignment we were tasked to develop a large-format printed 2D composition BUT not allowed to use any adobe software or other software explicitly designed for print design and production in our process. This poster is meant to represent the progression of gender roles and technology in relation to our childhood (the construction and destruction of norms) Software: (Google Drawing) run and screen shot games, scanning found items.

Group Poetry Poster

2023 - OCAD YEAR 2

For this project, I worked with a group of four other students to develop a shared InDesign poster template for a poetry event. Together, we defined key typographic elements including the event title, location, date, and list of poets‚Äîdrawing from poets we had previously researched. Once the group template was complete, I designed my own unique poster using the shared typographic system. I made creative decisions about imagery, color, and composition, integrating assets from a prior project along with new visual elements. The result was a cohesive series of five posters, each visually distinct yet unified through consistent typographic structure.

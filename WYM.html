How Symbolism, Aestheticism, and Decadence informed this Illustration
Symbolists artworks are marked with flat, abstract forms. They all emphasized the importance of imagination and emotions over realism and rationalism, disgust at the corruption and rampant materialism of the modern world and partly by a related desire to escape it into realms of the aesthetic, fantastic, erotic or religious. Typically, the influence was felt as an interest in pleasure, an interest in experimental sexuality, and a fascination with the bizarre, all packaged with a somewhat transgressive spirit and an aesthetic that values material excess. The decadent movement was characterized by self-disgust, delight in perversion, a cynical kind of hope, and employment of crude humor and a belief in the superiority of human creativity over logic and the natural world.

Madonna/Whore Dichotomy
The "Final Girl" is virginal, responsible, and the lone survivor. The horror genre favorite victims: women who survive because they're virtuous.

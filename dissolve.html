Bleed into the Blue

2021 - BEAL ART YEAR 2

I wanted to develop my creativity with distortion and the destruction of the image. These photographic chemical blurs became introspections of drug addiction and the feeling of losing yourself. This project aided and tested my understanding of different inks and papers as well as abrasive chemicals.

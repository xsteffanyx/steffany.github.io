Food is a Religion

2025 - OCAD YEAR 3

In this project, I developed a visual and auditory interpretation of a six-word story through both an animated collage and a printed postcard. I began by writing a six-word narrative and then created an art direction board in InDesign to guide my choices in imagery, typography, color, and sound. Using Adobe After Effects, I brought the story to life through a short animated film, combining visual rhythm, collage techniques, and sound effects to evoke mood and meaning.

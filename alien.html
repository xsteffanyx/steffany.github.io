Alien Infographics

2024 - OCAD YEAR 3

In this two-part project, I explored how design decisions impact the way data is understood and interpreted. For the first infographic, I created a clean, horizon graph chart that accurately communicated data on the topic of aliens. I focused on clarity by carefully labeling axes, including annotations, and providing a detailed caption with methods, definitions, and sources. For the second piece, I reinterpreted the same topic with a slightly different dataset to present a contrasting perspective‚Äîdemonstrating how visual emphasis, tone, and hierarchy can shift a message without altering the facts. Through this process, I critically examined the role of the designer in shaping narratives, and reflected on which version was more subjective and why, while proposing ways to improve objectivity.
